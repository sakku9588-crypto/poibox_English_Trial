📘 Quick Start Guide (Introduction)
1. Installation

Download the shorts_sync_protected.exe.

Prepare your YouTube Data API Key and Google Cloud JSON Key (credentials.json).

Launch the exe! (Trial version: 5 launches / 14 days).

2. Google Sheets Preparation

Create a Google Sheet to store your fan database.

Share the sheet with the email address found in your credentials.json.

Copy the Sheet URL into the app's settings.

3. Fetch & Sync

Paste your YouTube Channel ID.

Click "Fetch Video Titles" to load your latest Shorts.

Select the videos you want to analyze and click "Sync to Google Sheets".

4. Data Management

The app automatically records the User Name, Channel ID, Comment Text, and Date.

Duplicate entries are automatically handled to keep your database clean.
