📘 Quick Start Guide (Introduction)
1. Installation

Download the _protected.exe file.

Place your credentials.json (Google Cloud Service Account) in the same folder.

Launch the exe! (Trial version allows 5 launches / 14 days).

2. Google Sheets Setup

Create a Google Sheet and share it with the email address in your credentials.json.

Copy the Sheet URL and paste it into the "Sheet URL" field in the app.

3. OBS Integration

The app automatically generates obs_events.json.

Use your favorite OBS script or plugin to read this JSON for real-time alerts.

4. Start Streaming

Enter your YouTube Video ID.

Click "Start Monitoring" and enjoy your interactive stream!
